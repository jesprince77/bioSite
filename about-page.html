About Me

Jim Reichart grew up in rural Valley Falls, Kansas just west of Kansas City, Mo. Growing up on a farm with five other siblings he learned the importance of the land and how to use it for sustenance. As he got older he wanted to leave rural land-locked Kansas and set his sites on the East coast. Attending Regent University in Virginia Beach, he majored in English. In preparation for law school, he took a few pre-law courses. Health issues quickly changed his plans when he found out he had kidney cancer in his late twenties. Always inspired to help other people, Jim had to learn how to help himself beat cancer.

While recovering from a nephrectomy, to rid himself of cancer, he found useful holistic ways to keep himself cancer-free. He now celebrates 20 years free from cancer.

He found solace and inspiration from the teachings of Eckhart Tolle, Oprah Winfrey, and President Obama to name a few. Jim's mentors all promoted positive thinking and healthy lifestyles and fought against climate change. These valuable life lessons, along with his triumphant battle over cancer, led him to start Planet Possibility, a health and wellness blog dedicated to providing healthy, nutritional, and natural resources to better our lives.

This celebrates life. Educates the spirit and soul. Gives opportunity to those who feel like there is none. By providing options and healthy alternatives to unhealthy lifestyles that many Americans feel like they have no choice to live. These high-risk lifestyles increase the risks of cancer, diabetes, stroke, and many other ailments. Jim's desire is for Planet Possibility to reconnect us back with nature by promoting healthy living mentally, spiritually, and physically.
